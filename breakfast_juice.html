Breakfast Zinger Juice

Description

This is a delicious, cleansing juice that's great in the morning.

Great way to kick start your day, while getting necessary vitamins.

Ingredients

  • 2 lemons - peeled, seeded, and quartered
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 apples, quartered
  • 2 beets, trimmed and chopped

Steps

  1. Press lemons, carrots, apples, and beets through a juicer.
  2. Pour into a large glass with ice.
  3. Drink immediately.
