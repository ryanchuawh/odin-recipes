Super Immunity Boost Juice

Description

Orange, ginger, and turmeric, well-known immunity powerhouses, combine to make this

tasty and healthy juice that is loaded with Vitamin C, antioxidants, and anti-inflammatory properties.

Ingredients

  • 1 (2 inch) piece fresh ginger root
  • 2 (1 1/2 inch) pieces fresh turmeric
  • 3 large naval oranges, peeled

Steps

  1. Process ginger, turmeric, and oranges through a juicer in the order listed.
  2. Pour over ice.
  3. Drink immediately.
